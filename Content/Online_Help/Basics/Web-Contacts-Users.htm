Contacts and users

In ProjectSight, the following types of contacts and users are available.

  Access ProjectSight Comment on a record Manage project files and records
Company contacts
Project contacts 1 A project contact can add comments by replying to the notification email for an assignment or courtesy copy.
ProjectSight users 2 Your permissions determine which actions are available.
  • A company contact may or may not be a ProjectSight user or a project contact.
  • A project contact is always a company contact and may also be a ProjectSight user.
  • A ProjectSight user is always a company contact and may also be a project contact.
Company contacts

If you're a company contact, your name is included on the Contacts tab of a company record. You become a company contact when:

  • You're added to the Contacts tab of a company record.
    • Example

    Contacts tab of a company record

  • You're invited to a project.
    • Example

    New team member added to a company record

Project contacts

If you're a project contact, you have access to a project. You become a project contact when:

  • You're invited through the team member settings, the project banner, or a contact search dialog.
    • Example

    Invitation button on the banner

    Invitation button in a contact search dialog

  • The project is selected for you on the Contacts tab of the company record.
    • Example

    Projects cell on the Contacts tab of a company record

ProjectSight users

You become a ProjectSight user when:

  • A ProjectSight user invites you to collaborate in a project through the team member settings, the project banner, or a contact search dialog.
    • Example

    Invitation button on the banner

    Invitation button in a contact search dialog

  • You create a Trimble Identity account from the ProjectSight page and then sign in to ProjectSight.
